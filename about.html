About Medivo

Smarter Care. Better Outcomes.

Medivo is a health technology company building vibrant, connected digital solutions that help healthcare organizations deliver better care through more integrated workflows, better patient experience, and future-ready data capabilities.

Mission

To make healthcare delivery more connected, responsive, and human.

Medivo exists to reduce the friction between people, processes, and healthcare information. The company is building toward a future where digital care environments are easier to use, more intelligent, and better connected.

Core Values

Care, trust, energy, and innovation.

Medivo’s brand and product direction are built around people-centered care and long-term digital health capability.
